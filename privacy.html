Purpose of data processing

The collected personal data will be processed depending on the legal basis for processing, in to:

Personal data processed for the purposes previously mentioned will be processed until termination of the service provided by UbuBoo Games to the data subject or until the consent is withdrawn.

Personal data processed by UbuBoo Games will not be transferred outside European Economic Area (European Union countries and Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway).

  1. The right to access your personal data, rectify, delete or limit processing;

Exercise of the revocation right consent does not affect the processing that took place until the consent was withdrawn. Final provisions

This Privacy Policy applies from the date of publication, that is from February of 2021.
